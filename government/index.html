Why Choose a Government Career?

Job Security

Permanent civil service positions offer tenure protection. Once confirmed after probation, you cannot be removed without due process.

Full Benefits Package

GSIS retirement and life insurance, Pag-IBIG housing fund, PhilHealth, 13th month, mid-year bonus, clothing allowance, and PEI every December.

Clear Career Growth

Step increments every 3 years, promotion opportunities through the HRMPSB process, and SSL salary increases through each government tranche.
