About DepEd Hiring

The Department of Education (DepEd) is the largest government employer in the Philippines, with over 800,000 employees nationwide. It manages more than 47,000 public schools serving over 27 million learners from kindergarten through Senior High School.

DepEd conducts mass hiring annually — typically during the first half of the school year (February to June) for positions that will take effect at the start of the next school year in August.

All Teacher positions require a valid Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) license under RA 1080 (Licensure Examination for Teachers / LET). DepEd does not accept non-PRC-licensed teachers for permanent positions.

How to Apply for DepEd Jobs

1

Monitor Official DepEd Announcements

Visit deped.gov.ph and your target Schools Division Office (SDO) website for official vacancy announcements. DepEd NCR posts at depedncr.com.ph. Vacancies are also posted on the CSC Job Portal.

2

Prepare Your Complete Application Package

The HRMPSB (Human Resource Merit Promotion and Selection Board) is strict about completeness. Prepare: CSC Form 212 (PDS) with signature and photo, Official Transcript of Records (OTR, certified), Diploma (certified), PRC ID and Board Rating, NBI Clearance (within 6 months), and Medical Certificate from a government physician.

3

Submit to the SDO Before the Deadline

Submit personally or via registered mail to the Schools Division Superintendent's Office of your target division. Do NOT submit on the deadline day — submit at least 2–3 days before to ensure it is received and acknowledged.

4

Prepare for Demo Teaching and Panel Interview

Shortlisted applicants are invited for a 30-minute demo lesson and a structured panel interview. The demo is scored on content mastery, classroom management, and student engagement. The Comparative Assessment of Ranking (CAR) system weighs performance, education, and experience.

Standard Requirements for Teacher Positions

Required

  • Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd) or Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) — or any bachelor's degree with 18 units of professional education
  • Passed the Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET) — RA 1080 eligibility from PRC
  • Filipino citizen — no pending criminal or administrative case
  • Physically and mentally fit for teaching duties

Preferred (adds points in CAR ranking)

  • LET rating of 85% or above (higher ratings earn additional CAR points)
  • Relevant teaching experience or practicum records in the assigned subject area
  • Proficiency with ICT tools for blended and online learning
  • Postgraduate units in Education (for Master Teacher)

Key Facts About DepEd Employment

Hiring Cycle

DepEd mass hiring typically occurs in February–June for positions effective in August (start of school year). Emergency hiring may occur throughout the year for mid-year vacancies.

Total Compensation

Teacher I monthly take-home: approx. ₱30,059. Annual total compensation including 13th month, mid-year and year-end bonuses, PEI, and clothing allowance is approximately ₱430,000+/year.

Job Security

Permanent DepEd teachers cannot be removed from service without due process. After the 6-month probationary period, you have security of tenure under the Civil Service law.
