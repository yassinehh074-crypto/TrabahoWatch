Country Guide — Europe & Americas

🇨🇦

Canada

Nurses, Caregivers, Engineers — PR Pathway

Canada is the top destination for Filipino nurses seeking permanent residency. The Health Care Worker stream under Express Entry provides a direct PR pathway. NCLEX-RN registration required.

  • 💵 RN: CAD 35–45/hr (~₱1,400–₱1,800/hr)
  • 🏠 Province-specific licensing (NCLEX + CPRN)
  • 🛂 PR pathway via Express Entry (CRS 450+)
  • ✈️ Via DMW-accredited agencies or direct employer
🇬🇧

United Kingdom

NHS Nurses, Care Workers — Health and Care Visa

The NHS actively recruits Filipino nurses and healthcare workers through the Health and Care Worker visa. Processing time: 3–8 weeks. NMC registration required for nurses (OSCE exam in the UK).

  • 💵 RN Band 5: GBP 28,407–34,581/yr (~₱2.1M–₱2.6M)
  • 📋 NMC registration + OSCE exam on arrival
  • 🛂 Skilled Worker visa; family included
  • ✈️ UK is an ethical recruitment country (no ban)
🇺🇸

United States

EB-3 Green Card, H-2B, Travel Nurse

The US offers the highest nursing salaries globally. EB-3 immigrant visa sponsored by hospitals leads to a Green Card. NCLEX-RN + VisaScreen required. Priority Dates for Philippines are improving in 2026.

  • 💵 RN: USD 35–55/hr (~₱2,000–₱3,200/hr)
  • 📋 NCLEX-RN + CGFNS VisaScreen certificate
  • 🛂 EB-3 → Green Card (2–5 year wait for PH)
  • ✈️ Only via POEA-accredited US placement agencies
🇩🇪

Germany

Caregiver, Nurse — Skilled Immigration Act

Germany's Skilled Immigration Act 2023 opened new pathways for Filipino healthcare workers. The Triple Win program (GIZ-BA) offers structured placement with free German language training in PH.

  • 💵 Care Worker: EUR 2,200–2,800/mo (~₱132,000–₱168,000)
  • 📋 German B2 language level required
  • 🎓 Free German language training via GIZ-BA Triple Win
  • 🛂 Permanent residency after 4 years

🇨🇦 Canada Nurse — Best PR Pathway in 2026

The Health Care Worker stream under Express Entry offers 500 CRS points to healthcare workers with a job offer in Canada. Pass NCLEX-RN, get your CPRN registration, then apply. Total process: 12–24 months from PH to PR.

Why Consider Europe or the Americas?

Permanent Residency Path

Canada, UK, Germany, and Ireland all offer clear pathways to permanent residency for skilled Filipino healthcare workers — allowing you to bring your family and build a permanent life abroad.

Highest Global Salaries

A Filipino nurse in the USA earns 4–6× what a government nurse earns in the Philippines. Even after tax, take-home pay is significantly higher than Gulf destinations.

Strong Worker Protections

European and North American labour laws provide strong worker protections — minimum wage enforcement, overtime pay, union representation, and comprehensive social security benefits.
