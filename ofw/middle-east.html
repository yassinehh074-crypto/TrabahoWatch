Standard Gulf Employment Benefits

Tax-Free Income

Gulf countries impose no personal income tax. Your entire salary is take-home pay — no GSIS, PhilHealth, or income tax deductions.

Accommodation Provided

Most Gulf employers provide free or subsidized accommodation (compound housing) and meals or food allowance — significantly reducing living costs.

Annual Airfare Home

Standard Gulf contracts include one round-trip airfare to the Philippines per year. End-of-contract gratuity (1 month salary per year) is legally mandated.

For licensed professionals (nurses, engineers): Most Gulf countries require DataFlow credential verification before employment. Start the process early — DataFlow takes 4–8 weeks. DFA Apostille also required for documents.
