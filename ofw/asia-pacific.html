Asia Pacific Country Guide for OFWs

🇯🇵

Japan

Care Worker Program (EPA / TITP / SSW)

Japan's aging population has created massive demand for Filipino care workers. The Specified Skilled Worker (SSW) visa allows up to 5 years, with the SSW2 visa for indefinite stay.

  • 💴 Starting: ¥180,000–₱72,000/mo
  • 📋 Requires: N4 Japanese language (JLPT)
  • 🏠 Employer-provided accommodation typical
  • ✈️ Via POEA-accredited agencies or DOLE OWWA
🇸🇬

Singapore

Professionals, Nurses, Domestic Workers

Singapore is the top destination for Filipino professionals — engineers, nurses, IT specialists, and domestic helpers. S-Pass and Employment Pass for skilled workers.

  • 💵 Nurse: SGD 2,500–SGD 3,500 (~₱97,000–₱136,000)
  • 💵 Helper: SGD 600–800 (~₱23,000–₱31,000)
  • 📋 Requires: Professional license or degree
  • ✈️ 4-hour flight from Manila
🇰🇷

South Korea

EPS (Employment Permit System) — Manufacturing

Korea's EPS program is government-to-government (DOLE-HRD Korea). Requires passing the EPS-TOPIK (Korean language test). Manufacturing and agriculture sectors. No placement fee charged.

  • 💵 KRW 2,010,580/mo minimum (~₱78,000)
  • 📋 Requires: EPS-TOPIK exam (Korean language)
  • 🚫 ZERO placement fee (G2G program)
  • ✈️ Apply via POEA EPS Korea desk
🇭🇰

Hong Kong

Domestic Helper — Standard Employment Contract

Hong Kong employs over 200,000 Filipino domestic helpers under a standard government contract. Minimum Allowable Wage (MAW) is reviewed annually. Free on rest days, live-in arrangement.

  • 💵 HKD 5,373/mo MAW (~₱38,000)
  • 🏠 Employer must provide accommodation + meals
  • ✈️ Annual airfare to Philippines
  • 📋 2-year contract, renewable
