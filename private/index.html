Why the Private Sector?

Faster Salary Growth

Private companies offer performance-based raises, annual salary reviews, and promotion cycles based on merit — not just seniority or step increments.

HMO for You and Family

Most large private companies offer HMO coverage for employees and dependents — a benefit that can be worth ₱60,000–₱120,000 per year for a family of four.

Remote and Hybrid Options

Many Philippine private sector roles — especially in tech, finance, and BPO back-office — now offer hybrid or fully remote work arrangements.

TESDA Certification — Your Shortcut to Employment

For skilled trades and technical roles

View Trades Jobs →

NC I — Entry Level

National Certificate I covers basic technical competencies. Takes 2–3 months. Opens doors to helper and assistant roles in construction, manufacturing, and services.

NC II — Industry Standard

The most in-demand certification. Required for electricians, welders, plumbers, and care workers. Also qualifies holders for Gulf construction deployment.

TWSP Scholarship

TESDA's Training for Work Scholarship Program (TWSP) covers tuition for priority courses. Apply through tesda.gov.ph — enrollment is free for qualified applicants.
