Submit a Job Listing

Know a verified vacancy TrabahoWatch hasn't covered yet? Help fellow Filipinos find it. We review all submissions within 48 hours and publish eligible listings for free.

What We Accept

  • Government job vacancies with an official source URL
  • Private company openings posted on an official company careers page
  • OFW opportunities through DMW-accredited agencies with a verifiable source
  • Positions with a deadline that has not yet passed

What We Do Not Accept

  • Paid promotions or sponsored listings of any kind
  • Unverified jobs without an official source URL
  • OFW opportunities from non-DMW-accredited agencies
  • Jobs with deadlines that have already passed

What Happens After You Submit

1

We verify your source

We open your source URL and confirm the vacancy is real, active, and from an official channel.

2

We write or update the listing

Using your submission and the official source, we create a complete listing following our editorial standards.

3

Published — free for all

The listing goes live for every Filipino jobseeker to find. If you requested credit, your name appears on the listing.

Review Timeline

Most submissions are reviewed within 24–48 hours. High-priority listings (urgent deadlines, high-demand sectors) are typically reviewed within a few hours.

Not all submissions will be published. We may decline submissions that cannot be verified, are from non-official sources, or do not meet our editorial standards. We do not publish unverified or duplicate listings.
