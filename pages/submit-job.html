✓ What We Accept

  • ✓ Government plantilla positions (CSC-posted)
  • ✓ Private company jobs with official career page URL
  • ✓ OFW jobs through DMW-accredited agencies with job order
  • ✓ Positions with verifiable source URL
  • ✓ Jobs with clear salary or compensation information

✗ What We Don't Accept

  • ✗ Jobs without an official, verifiable source URL
  • ✗ Freelance, per-project, or home-based gig work
  • ✗ MLM, networking, or commission-only positions
  • ✗ OFW jobs from non-DMW-accredited agencies
  • ✗ Positions that appear to be scams or illegal recruitment
  • ✗ Duplicate submissions already listed on TrabahoWatch

Review Timeline

Submitted vacancies are reviewed within 2–5 business days. Verified listings are published with full salary breakdown, requirements, and step-by-step application guide. We may contact you for additional information. Not all submissions are guaranteed to be published.

Always Free

Submitting a vacancy to TrabahoWatch is completely free. We never charge employers, HR officers, or community members for listing job openings. TrabahoWatch earns through Google AdSense only.
