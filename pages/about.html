About TrabahoWatch

Free job information for every Filipino — government, private, and OFW. No registration. No fees. No gatekeeping.

What is TrabahoWatch?

TrabahoWatch is a free Philippine job information platform. We publish detailed, verified information about job openings across three sectors: government (civil service), private sector, and OFW (Overseas Filipino Worker) opportunities.

We are not a recruitment agency and we do not process job applications. We are an information resource — like a Wikipedia for Philippine employment — where every Filipino can find honest, well-researched job information without creating an account, paying a subscription, or being marketed to.

Our mission is simple: makapagbigay ng tamang impormasyon tungkol sa trabaho para sa bawat Pilipino — to give every Filipino the right information about jobs so they can make the best decision for themselves and their families.

Why We Built TrabahoWatch

Finding a government job in the Philippines should not require navigating dozens of different agency websites, comparing salary grades from a 2019 DBM circular, or paying a JobStreet premium subscription just to see full salary details. And figuring out if an OFW opportunity is legitimate — or a scam — should not require insider knowledge.

We noticed that the information exists — it's on CSC, on agency websites, in DMW job orders, in Executive Orders — but it's scattered, inconsistent, and often inaccessible to the average Filipino job seeker who may be browsing on a mobile phone with limited data.

TrabahoWatch puts it all in one place. Every government job listing includes the official salary grade (SSL VI), the exact deductions (GSIS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG), the take-home estimate, the requirements, the documents, and the step-by-step application process — sourced directly from official government channels.

For OFW seekers, we explain the DMW accreditation process, the maximum legal placement fee, the DataFlow requirement for Saudi Arabia, the NCLEX for Canada — the practical knowledge that helps Filipinos deploy safely and avoid illegal recruiters.

What Makes TrabahoWatch Different

100% Free. Always.

No premium tier. No salary details hidden behind a paywall. No account required to see full job information. Every Filipino deserves equal access to job information regardless of income.

Verified Government Data

All salary data sourced from official EOs, DBM circulars, and SSL VI schedules. All government job requirements cross-referenced with CSC memoranda and agency HR standards.

Mobile-First Design

Built for the Filipino job seeker browsing on a prepaid mobile connection. Fast loading on 3G, readable on small screens, works offline after first visit (PWA-enabled).

OFW Safety First

Every OFW listing links to DMW verification. We prominently display the DMW hotline (1348), maximum legal placement fees, and red flags for illegal recruitment — because safe deployment is non-negotiable.

Who We Serve

TrabahoWatch is for every Filipino looking for honest job information — regardless of where they are in their career journey:

  • Fresh graduates figuring out whether to take a government exam or start in the private sector — and what each path actually pays.
  • Public school teachers and nurses checking whether the government salary is actually higher than the private hospital offering them a job. (It often is — but now you can verify it yourself.)
  • BPO workers wondering if they should try for a government position or go OFW, and what the real salary difference looks like after deductions.
  • Aspiring OFWs trying to understand the deployment process, verify if their agency is legit, and know what salary they should realistically expect in Saudi Arabia, Japan, or Canada.
  • Parents, relatives, and community leaders helping someone in their family navigate the job market for the first time.

How TrabahoWatch Is Funded

TrabahoWatch is funded entirely through Google AdSense display advertising. Ads are served automatically by Google based on page content and visitor interests — we do not control which specific ads appear.

We want to be completely transparent about this:

  • No employer or recruiter pays to be featured on TrabahoWatch. Our listings are selected independently based on public availability and relevance.
  • Advertising revenue does not influence our editorial decisions. We have never removed, modified, or suppressed a job listing for commercial reasons.
  • You can use an ad blocker and TrabahoWatch will still work perfectly. We would prefer you didn't — ads are how we keep the lights on — but we respect your choice.
