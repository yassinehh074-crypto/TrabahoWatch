1. Who We Are

TrabahoWatch ("we," "us," or "our") is an independent Philippine job information website operated at trabahowatch.com. We provide free, verified job information for Filipino jobseekers — government, private sector, and OFW opportunities. We are not a recruitment agency and do not process job applications.

For the purposes of the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations, TrabahoWatch is the Personal Information Controller with respect to data collected through this website.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Voluntarily

We collect personal data only when you choose to provide it:

  • Contact form: Name, email address, and the content of your message when you contact us through our contact form. Subject category and any job URL you provide.
  • Newsletter subscription: Email address only, when you subscribe to our job alert newsletter.
  • Job submission form: Job details and optionally your name and email if you submit a job listing for review.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

When you visit TrabahoWatch, our servers and third-party analytics tools may automatically collect:

  • Log data: IP address (anonymized where possible), browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, time and date of visit.
  • Device data: Screen resolution, device type (mobile/desktop), preferred language setting.
  • Cookies: Small text files placed on your device. See Section 4 for details.

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, request, or store:

  • Resumes, CVs, or job application documents
  • Government-issued ID numbers (SSS, PhilSys, TIN, PRC)
  • Financial information, bank account, or credit card numbers
  • Passwords (we have no user accounts)
  • Biometric data of any kind

4. Cookies and Tracking

TrabahoWatch uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience and enable third-party services.

4.1 Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the site to function and cannot be disabled:

  • theme — Stores your dark/light mode preference (localStorage). Not a cookie, never sent to servers.
  • session — Standard browser session data for page rendering. Expires when you close your browser.

4.2 Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use TrabahoWatch — which pages are most helpful, where visitors come from, and how to improve content. Google Analytics uses anonymized IP addresses and does not personally identify you.

You can opt out of Google Analytics across all sites at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout .

4.3 Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense places cookies to serve contextual advertisements. These cookies may collect data about your browsing behavior across sites to serve relevant ads. See Section 5 for details.

4.4 How to Control Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality. Most modern browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all third-party cookies
  • Enable "Do Not Track" signals

5. Google AdSense

TrabahoWatch displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to TrabahoWatch and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings .

All advertisements on TrabahoWatch are clearly labeled "Advertisement". Job listings on TrabahoWatch are never paid placements — they are published based solely on editorial criteria. Advertisers have no influence over our job content.

For more information on Google's data practices, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites that use its services .

6. Newsletter and Email Alerts

If you subscribe to TrabahoWatch job alerts, we collect your email address for the sole purpose of sending you updates about new job listings relevant to your interests.

We use Buttondown or Brevo (third-party email service providers) to manage and send newsletters. Your email is stored on their servers in accordance with their privacy policies. We do not share your email with any other third party.

You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any newsletter email, or by contacting us at hello@trabahowatch.com . We will remove your email within 5 business days of request.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected:

  • Contact form messages: Retained in our email inbox for up to 12 months, then deleted. Formspree retains submissions for 30 days.
  • Newsletter emails: Retained until you unsubscribe. Deleted within 5 business days of unsubscribe request.
  • Analytics data: Retained by Google Analytics for 26 months (standard retention period), anonymized.
  • Server logs: Retained by our hosting provider (Vercel) for up to 30 days for security purposes.

9. Your Rights Under RA 10173

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations, you have the following rights as a data subject:

Right to Be Informed

You have the right to be informed about how we collect, use, and store your personal data. This Privacy Policy fulfills that right.

Right to Access

You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you at any time, free of charge. We will respond within 15 calendar days of receiving your request.

Right to Correction

If any personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you may request correction. We will update the data within 15 calendar days.

Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

You may request that we delete any personal data we hold about you — including newsletter subscription and contact form data. We will delete within 5 business days, except where retention is required by law.

Right to Object

You may object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes (newsletter) at any time by unsubscribing. You may also object to processing based on legitimate interest by contacting our DPO.

Right to Data Portability

You may request a machine-readable copy of personal data you have provided to us (e.g., contact form data, newsletter email). We will provide it in JSON or CSV format within 15 days.

Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe your data privacy rights have been violated, you may file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) of the Philippines at privacy.gov.ph or call the NPC hotline: (02) 8234-2228.

To exercise any of the above rights, email us at privacy@trabahowatch.com with the subject "Data Privacy Request" and we will respond within 15 calendar days.

10. Children's Privacy

TrabahoWatch does not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18 years of age. Our content is intended for adults seeking employment information. If we discover we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@trabahowatch.com .
