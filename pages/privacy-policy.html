Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026  ·  Effective: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TrabahoWatch ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit trabahowatch.com (the "Website"). By using this Website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree, please discontinue use of the Website.

1. Who We Are

TrabahoWatch is an independent, privately operated Philippine job information website. We are not affiliated with the Civil Service Commission (CSC), the Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE), the Department of Migrant Workers (DMW), or any Philippine government agency.

We are not a recruitment agency. We do not process job applications, charge placement fees, or collect resumes for employment purposes.

Website: trabahowatch.com
Contact: trabahowatch.com/pages/contact.html
Country of operation: Republic of the Philippines

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact Form: Your name, email address, and message content when you send us a message through our Contact page. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Job Submission Form: Employer name, job details, and contact information when you submit a job vacancy for publication. Used only to verify and publish the submitted listing.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit TrabahoWatch, the following information is collected automatically through standard web technologies:

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, time spent on pages, and date/time of visit. This data is collected by our hosting provider (Vercel) and Google Analytics.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Small text files placed on your device. Full details in Section 5 (Cookies Policy).
  • Device Information: Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop), screen resolution, and approximate geographic location (country/city level — derived from IP address, not GPS).

2.3 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

TrabahoWatch stores the following data in your browser's localStorage — this data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers:

  • Theme preference (light or dark mode): key trabahowatch-theme
  • Recent search history in the search overlay: key trabahowatch-recent-searches (stores last 5 search queries typed by you)
  • Scroll position for job map page (temporary session data, cleared on navigation): key trabahowatch-loadmore

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for trabahowatch.com.

4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

TrabahoWatch uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is an advertising service provided by Google LLC (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA).

How Google AdSense Works

Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites across the internet. This is known as interest-based advertising or remarketing.

Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve more relevant ads and to limit the number of times you see an ad.

What Data Google AdSense Collects

  • Cookie identifiers to recognize returning visitors
  • Pages you visit on trabahowatch.com (to serve contextually relevant ads)
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city) for geo-targeted advertising
  • Browser and device type for ad rendering optimization
TrabahoWatch does not have access to or control over the cookies used by Google AdSense. These cookies are managed entirely by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of Google's use of cookies for interest-based advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ad Settings (adssettings.google.com)
  • Network Advertising Initiative opt-out (optout.aboutads.info)
  • NAI opt-out page (optout.networkadvertising.org)

Note: Opting out of personalized ads does not mean you will not see ads. You will still see ads on TrabahoWatch, but they will be less tailored to your browsing history.

Google Privacy Policy

For more information on how Google collects and processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy .

5. Google Analytics

TrabahoWatch uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymized data about your visit including pages viewed, session duration, bounce rate, traffic source, and device type.

IP Anonymization: We have enabled IP anonymization in Google Analytics. This means your full IP address is never stored by Google Analytics — the last octet of your IP address is masked before any data is recorded.

Data Retention: Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months (our configured retention period) before automatic deletion.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by:

  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Enabling "Do Not Track" (DNT) in your browser settings — we honor DNT signals
  • Using a browser with built-in tracker blocking (Firefox, Brave, Safari with ITP enabled)

6. Cookies Policy

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device by websites you visit. Cookies allow websites to recognize your browser and remember information about your visit.

Types of Cookies Used on TrabahoWatch

Cookie Type Source Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary TrabahoWatch Security, session management, CSRF protection. Cannot be disabled. Session
Analytics Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat) Track website usage statistics (pages, sessions, bounce rate). 2 years (_ga); 24 hours (_gid)
Advertising Google AdSense (IDE, ANID, DSID, FLC, AID, TAID, _gads) Serve relevant ads, limit ad frequency, measure ad performance. Up to 2 years
Preference TrabahoWatch (localStorage) Remember your dark/light mode choice and recent searches. Persistent (until cleared)
CDN / Performance Vercel, Google Fonts Optimize content delivery speed and font loading performance. Session to 1 year

How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of TrabahoWatch (e.g., your theme preference will not be remembered).

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari (iOS): Settings → Safari → Privacy and Security

TrabahoWatch contains links to external websites including government portals (csc.gov.ph, doh.gov.ph, pnp.gov.ph, dmwph.com), company career pages, and other informational resources.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites. When you click on an external link, you leave TrabahoWatch and your activity is governed by that website's own privacy policy. We recommend reading the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

TrabahoWatch is intended for individuals who are 18 years of age or older — the minimum working age in most regulated employment in the Philippines.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately using the contact form and we will promptly delete such information.

Our website does not contain content directed at minors and is not designed to appeal to children.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption on all pages — all data transmitted between your browser and trabahowatch.com is encrypted in transit

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

11. Your Rights Under Philippine Data Privacy Act

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act 10173), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to be Informed: You have the right to know what personal data we collect and how it is used. (This Privacy Policy fulfills that obligation.)
  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing, profiling, or statistical purposes.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to obtain a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) of the Philippines at privacy.gov.ph if you believe your data rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our contact page. We will respond within 30 days as required by the Data Privacy Act.

12. Notice for European Union Visitors (GDPR)

If you are visiting TrabahoWatch from the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Our legal bases for processing your data are:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): For advertising cookies (Google AdSense) and preference cookies
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): For website analytics (Google Analytics) and security functions
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): For processing contact form submissions in order to respond to your inquiry

EU visitors may also lodge complaints with their local Data Protection Authority (DPA).
