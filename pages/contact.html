Response Time

We respond to all messages within 1–2 business days (Monday–Friday, Philippine time). Corrections to verified errors are applied within 24 hours on weekdays.

We Can Help With:

  • ✓ Job listing errors (wrong salary, closed position)
  • ✓ Salary grade or SSL VI data corrections
  • ✓ Questions about government job requirements
  • ✓ OFW deployment information queries
  • ✓ Website feedback and feature suggestions
  • ✓ Partnership and media inquiries

We Cannot Help With:

  • ✗ Processing job applications (apply directly to employers)
  • ✗ OFW deployment (contact DMW or a licensed agency)
  • ✗ Legal or immigration advice
  • ✗ Resume writing or career coaching services
