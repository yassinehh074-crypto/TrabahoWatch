What to Expect

48-Hour Response

We read every message. For urgent job corrections or errors, we prioritize same-day review.

Error Reports Are Prioritized

If you find incorrect salary data, a broken link, or an expired listing that should be archived — we treat these as high-priority and fix them within 24–48 hours.

Your Data Is Safe

We use your email only to respond to your message. We do not add it to any mailing list without your explicit opt-in.

Common Topics

Found an outdated listing?

Select "Report an error" and paste the job URL. We'll verify and archive or update within 24 hours.

Want to submit a job?

Use the dedicated Submit a Job form — it is faster and goes directly to our content team.

Partnership inquiries?

Email hello@trabahowatch.com with subject: "Partnership". We respond to all serious inquiries within 3 business days.

TrabahoWatch is not a recruitment agency.

We cannot process your job application, forward your resume to employers, or arrange interviews. For job applications, please apply directly through the official channels listed in each job listing. If someone is contacting you claiming to represent TrabahoWatch for recruitment purposes — that is not us. Please report it immediately.
